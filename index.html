Frequently Asked Questions

Our focus is not simply making AI do more work. It is helping people work more clearly, safely and intelligently with AI.

What is Satoru Solutions?

Satoru Solutions provides AI coaching, training and practical implementation support. We help people understand their work, improve their processes and adopt AI at a pace they can manage.

Our current focus is Technical Coaching, Agile Coaching, Personal AI knowledge systems, local AI deployment and workflow intelligence.

Is Satoru an AI agent that replaces employees?

No. Our starting point is not replacing people.

AI may automate suitable tasks, but responsibility, risk and accountability remain with people. We use AI to improve understanding, judgment, consistency and confidence before increasing automation.

Why not automate an entire workflow immediately?

Real work is not the same as software code. Some digital tasks can be repeated, tested or rolled back easily. Client confirmation, payment, contractual approval and external submission may be difficult or impossible to undo.

A reliable workflow must distinguish between safe automation, expected waiting, abnormal delay, verification points and irreversible actions.

What is an AI Technical Coach?

A Technical Coach watches the work with you and guides you one step at a time. It can explain what is on the screen, identify the next action, recognise normal waiting time, detect likely errors and help diagnose problems.

The aim is not to hide the process from the user. It is to help the user understand and complete it confidently.

What is an AI Agile Coach?

An AI Agile Coach helps a person or team make work visible, improve collaboration and learn from experience. It can identify delays, repeated rework, unclear responsibilities, unnecessary handovers and weak decision points.

It does not force every organisation into the same framework or create cross-functional teams without clear purpose, authority and accountability.

How is an AI Coach different from an AI Assistant or AI Agent?
  • AI Assistant: helps prepare or complete a task.
  • AI Agent: performs a task on your behalf.
  • AI Coach: helps you understand the task, make better decisions and improve your capability.

These approaches can work together. Coaching establishes understanding and trust; carefully selected automation can follow later.

What parts of work can be automated safely?

Low-risk and repeatable tasks are usually the best starting point: searching, organising information, drafting, summarising, formatting, checking, data extraction and preparing documents for review.

Higher-risk actions should include checkpoints, approval, version history and a clear recovery method.

How do checkpoints and human approval work?

Each step can be assessed by asking:

  • Can this action be undone?
  • Does it change the outside world?
  • Is another person responsible for approving it?
  • Should the current state be saved first?
  • Does it require a second check?

Sending an email, confirming payment, signing a contract or submitting official information should not be treated like editing a temporary file.

Can Satoru help us understand a process before automating it?

Yes. We can first observe and map how work is actually performed, including decisions, waiting periods, handovers, risks, exceptions and repeated problems.

This makes the process easier to manage even before any automation is introduced.

What is workflow intelligence?

Workflow intelligence means that AI understands where work currently stands, what should happen next, what is waiting, who is responsible and when attention is required.

The AI does not need to perform every task to provide value. Better visibility alone can reduce confusion, unnecessary follow-up and avoidable rework.

Can Satoru build a Personal AI or private knowledge system?

Yes. A Personal AI can connect a user's permitted documents, email, conversations, spreadsheets and other knowledge so that information can be searched and understood in context.

We favour local-first and privacy-aware designs where the user keeps control of their data, logic and long-term knowledge.

Do you work with OpenAI, Claude and local AI models?

Yes. Different models are suitable for different jobs. A solution may use OpenAI, Claude, other cloud services or a locally deployed model depending on capability, privacy, cost and reliability requirements.

We focus on choosing and coordinating the right tools rather than locking every workflow to one model.

It is particularly useful where judgment, learning, client relationships, risk and accountability still matter.
